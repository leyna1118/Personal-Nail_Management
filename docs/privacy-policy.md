# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-03-23**

## Nail Management App

This privacy policy describes how the Nail Management app ("the App") handles user data.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data or device data. All data you enter (gel information, nail styles, images, inventory records) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Network Access

The App does **not** connect to the internet. It does not make any network requests and does not send data to any server or third-party service.

## Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. If you uninstall the App, all data will be deleted. You may use the App's built-in backup/restore feature to export your data to a file on your device.

## Permissions

The App does not request any special device permissions. Image selection is handled through the system file picker.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/leyna1118/Personal-Nail_Management).
